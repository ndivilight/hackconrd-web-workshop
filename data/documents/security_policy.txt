================================================================================
                    TECHCORP INFORMATION SECURITY POLICY
                         Effective Date: January 2026
================================================================================

1. PURPOSE
----------
This policy establishes the security requirements for all TechCorp employees,
contractors, and third parties who access company information systems.

2. SCOPE
--------
This policy applies to all information assets owned or managed by TechCorp.

3. PASSWORD POLICY
------------------
- All passwords must be unique and not reused across systems
- Password managers are strongly recommended
- Multi-factor authentication (MFA) is required for all critical systems

4. DATA CLASSIFICATION
----------------------
- PUBLIC: Information that can be freely shared
- INTERNAL: For employee use only
- CONFIDENTIAL: Sensitive business information
- RESTRICTED: Highly sensitive (PII, financial data)

5. INCIDENT REPORTING
---------------------
Report all security incidents immediately to: security@techcorp.local
Emergency hotline: 555-0199

6. ACCEPTABLE USE
-----------------
Company systems should be used primarily for business purposes.
Limited personal use is acceptable during breaks.

================================================================================
                    COMPLIANCE IS MANDATORY FOR ALL EMPLOYEES
================================================================================
